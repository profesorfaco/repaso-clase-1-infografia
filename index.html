ADOLF DEHN

Victoria Balbontin

Adolphe Dehn (1895 to 1968) was particularly associated with campaigns for social justice, and with caricatures such as New Masses. He did much of his work in lithography so these painterly colour studies are unusual.

These interviews are part of the Archives of American Art Oral History Program, started in 1958 to document the history of the visual arts in the United States, primarily through interviews with artists, historians, dealers, critics and others.
